Tafelspitz

Despite looking awful..well to be fair it was fairly awful. I think this was mainly my fault as I cooked it badly. Was really watery broth, I didn't grind the pepper. It was a fairly delicate (watery) flavour, but I'm assuming it wasn't meant to be.

Ingredients

  • 2Kg Beef
  • Marrow Bones
  • 3 Carrots
  • 3 Turnips
  • 1 Celeriac
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 10 Whole Peppercorns
  • 10 Whole All Spice
  • 10 Whole Juniper Berries

Instructions

  1. Chop up the veggies (not the onion) into 'rustic' chunks
  2. Put the Bones and meat into a pot, add 5 litres of boiling water, and blanch the shit out of them!
  3. Cut the onion in half, and dry fry! Should be borderline burned, not completely black
  4. Remove the scum from the Bones/Meat, and add the onion and spices. Do not add the salt for some reason. God forbid any flavour in this dish
  5. Simmer for an hour
  6. Add the rest of the veggies, and simmer for a further 45 mins.
  7. Once the meat is cooked, remove and let it rest. Remove veggies, sieve the liquid, then re-add the veggies. Serve the soup and meat separately, with some potatoes or some carby veg.

Verdict!

I think I very likely butchered a classic national dish, but the way I cooked it was awful. Not inedible but not good. 3/10
